She is empathetic and curious by nature as an artistic individual, thus sensitive to details and human psychology. She is interested in designing with a critical and practical mindset, and open-minded to be imaginative and embrace designing boundary-pushing systems for the future.

In her spare time, she is a passionate singer and dancer. She loves to express her optimistic, energetic, and driven attitude through singing and dancing and hopes to bring the positive energy to everyone around her.
